Hey little guys! Welcome to Gratidude. Glad to have you here.

The inspiration for this blog came from a quote from political theorist, Jose Ortega y Gassett:

"Everything in the world is strange and marvelous to well-open eyes.

The purpose of this compilation of random lists, rankings and thoughts is to give nods and notice to some of my favorite things in life; and perhaps help you add even more good things to your existence. I don't feel as if I have a particularly special lifestyle, and that is why I feel qualified to write about the simple, strange, and marvelous things that make me a grati-dude.
